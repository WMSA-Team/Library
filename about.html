About Us

WMSALibrary was founded in 2021, is a public library Online serving the people who love reading. Our library serves reading lovers, so we have established an online library, Through our library, we offer some books of various types. We have scientific and historical books, poetry books and some novels. Also, everyone can borrow books and buy them if he wants to own the book for himself.
